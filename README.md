# Universal-Travel-Adapter-Market-Research

# Market Feasibility: Universal Travel Adapter

## Project Overview
A data-driven study comparing the profitability of launching a new product on Amazon UK versus local platforms like Daraz to determine the best path for business growth.

## Key Actions
* **Market Comparison:** Used sales data and competitor analysis to evaluate the potential of both platforms.
* **Financial Modeling:** Conducted break-even analysis and sales forecasting to predict potential revenue.
* **Launch Strategy:** Created a 6-month roadmap, including pricing strategies and marketing steps to ensure the product becomes profitable.

## Business Impact
The project provided a clear, evidence-based recommendation that Amazon UK offered better long-term scalability and profitability for this specific product.

## Tools Used
* **Data Analysis:** Used Excel for sales forecasting and financial modeling.
* **Market Research:** Gathered competitive data to shape the launch strategy.
